The Don Pepin Garcia Cuban Classic, also known as the "Black Label," is a full-bodied cigar crafted in Esteli, Nicaragua. 

**Tasting Notes:**
Initially, there is a signature peppery start. I found some oakiness, but this cigar felt like a more complex cigarette with notes of leather and pepper. It didn't leave me staring at the night sky in amazement, but it's a very nice stick for the price ($7.69 - $9.40).
