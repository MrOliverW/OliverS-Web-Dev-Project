**Tasting Notes:**
Initially, there is a mild-barnyard funk to the stick that leaves an impression on the nose. Lighting it up by the ocean at low tide was amazing. 

Once lit, the aromas developed into one of the most delicious flavors I've experienced. It reminds me of toffee and moist wet fields at the peak of springtime, with an intense Brazilian-style chocolate/coffee tone. The muskiness makes the entire experience notable. Truly a piece of craft.
